Setup checklist

1. LinkedIn Developer App

Tacit app created at developer.linkedin.com.

2. Redirect URL

Add http://127.0.0.1:8000/linkedin/callback in the Auth tab.

3. Required products

Share on LinkedIn (personal posts) + Community Management API (company page posts). Request both in the Products tab — approval is usually same-day.

4. Company page admin

You must be a Super Admin or Content Admin of the TOGA page on LinkedIn for company page publishing to work.

Scope reference

w_member_social

Publish to personal profile. Included in Share on LinkedIn product.

w_organization_social

Publish to company pages. Included in Community Management API product.

r_organization_social

Auto-detect company pages you admin after connecting. Also in Community Management API.
