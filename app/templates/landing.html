FAQ

Questions before you start?

What is Tacit?

Tacit is a Knowledge-to-Content Platform for LinkedIn. It turns documents, reports, and expertise into content ideas, drafts, and scheduled posts — automatically.

Is Tacit just another AI writer?

No. Generic AI writers start from blank prompts. Tacit starts from your own expertise and source material, then matches your writing voice. The result sounds like you — not like AI.

What file types can I upload?

Tacit supports PDF, DOCX, TXT, and MD files. You can also paste raw text or create a marketing campaign directly from a brief.

Who is Tacit for?

Consultants, founders, engineers, reliability professionals, and subject-matter experts — anyone with deep knowledge who wants a consistent LinkedIn presence without becoming a full-time creator.

Does Tacit publish directly to LinkedIn?

Yes. Connect your LinkedIn account via OAuth and Tacit will publish approved, scheduled posts automatically. Nothing goes live without your review and approval.
